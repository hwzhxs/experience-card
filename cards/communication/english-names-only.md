---
title: Always use English names for colleagues
created: 2026-04-09
source: correction (multiple incidents)
---

All output — daily logs, people updates, project notes, Teams messages, Slack messages, conversation replies — must use English names only. Never use Chinese names (红军→Hongjun, 山鹰→Shanying). No exceptions. This rule exists because mixed naming causes confusion in searchable records.
